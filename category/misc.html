An experiment in blogging, coding, and living.

I've never been one for going the extra mile, learning in my free time, or following through with a hobby for very long. Soon I'm hoping for this to change though. After some remodeling in my office over the next month, I plan on utilizing my space more and more to learn and hopefully excel.

Planned projects atm are along the lines of:

  1. NTBD - a rewrite of www.750words.com. I really like the idea, but I'd like to have my own version of it running, and not hosted on someone else's server.

  3. A friend and I are throwing around ideas for a website for managing recipes and current inventory of what you have available at your house to make said recipe.

All in all, who knows where this will go, I just hope it's worth it.
